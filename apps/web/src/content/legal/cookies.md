# Cookie settings

> **Draft, not yet in effect.** Pending legal review. See the
> [legal overview](/legal).

**Last updated:** 2026-06-15

The Brika store keeps cookies to a strict minimum. We use **one essential cookie**
and no advertising, analytics, or cross-site tracking cookies, so there is nothing
to opt out of.

## The one cookie we set

| Cookie | Purpose | Type | Lifetime |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `session` | Keeps you signed in after GitHub OAuth. It is signed (HMAC) and read only by the store. | Essential | Until you sign out or it expires |

Because this cookie is strictly necessary to provide the signed-in experience you
asked for, it does not require consent and cannot be disabled while you are logged
in. If you sign out, or clear it in your browser, you are simply logged out.

## What we do not use

- No advertising or marketing cookies.
- No third-party analytics or fingerprinting.
- No cross-site or social tracking pixels.

## Managing cookies

You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Clearing the
`session` cookie logs you out of the store; nothing else depends on it.

For the full picture of what data we process, see the
[Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy).
